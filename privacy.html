Privacy Assurance

Privacy Policy

Learn how your personal details, complaints, and student records are handled.

1. Local Storage Architecture

The College Complaint & Feedback System runs entirely inside your web browser. All data you submit—including your login details, passwords, text of complaints, and resolved items—is written directly to your browser's local sandbox (localStorage and sessionStorage).

Crucially, no remote server or external database receives or monitors this information. This ensures absolute student privacy; your data never leaves your personal device.

2. Data Collection & Usage

The system stores minimal parameters strictly to facilitate the application's feedback routing mechanisms:

  • Account Authentication: Student USNs and passwords are kept in local storage to simulate verification and enable personalized history tracking.
  • Submissions: Feedback categories, targets (faculty, hostels, surroundings), titles, messages, and timestamps.
  • Theme Preferences: Dark/light mode settings are stored to ensure styling stays consistent across visits.

3. Clearing Data

Since your files are local, you have full control over them. You can delete all accounts, complaints, and preferences instantly by doing any of the following:

  • Clearing your web browser history/cookies (specifically choosing "Cookies and site data").
  • Opening browser inspector consoles and executing localStorage.clear().
  • Uninstalling or resetting the web browser application.
