Terms of Service

Please read these rules carefully before using the feedback and complaint system.

1. Purpose of the System

The College Complaint & Feedback System provides a safe, local space for students to document issues relating to subject teachers, faculty, surroundings, hostel infrastructure, and general academic services. It is designed to assist college administrations in maintaining high standards of quality and service.

2. Academic Honesty & Respect

In using this platform, you agree to respect academic staff and administration. You promise that all submissions will be:

  • Truthful and Objective: Submissions must reflect actual incidents, grades, or infrastructure faults. Fabricating data to damage reputation is strictly forbidden.
  • Free from Harassment: Feedback must use constructive, civil language. Personal attacks, insults, hate speech, or derogatory remarks targeting specific staff members are prohibited.

3. Responsibility & Security

Although data is kept local, you are responsible for maintaining the privacy of your student credentials (USN & Password). Do not share login credentials with other individuals. If you identify a bug or vulnerability in the client-side system, please notify a development coordinator immediately.
